Smartwatch FAQ

How do I charge my smartwatch?
To charge your smartwatch, use the provided charging cable and connect it to a power source or a USB port on your computer.
Is the smartwatch water-resistant?
Yes, the smartwatch is water-resistant. It is designed to withstand everyday activities like hand washing and rain. However, it is not suitable for swimming or submersion in water.
Can I change the watch band?
Yes, the smartwatch allows you to change the watch band. It is designed with interchangeable bands, giving you the flexibility to customize the look of your device.
