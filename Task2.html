ABOUT THE PRODUCT

  • Display: The Watch features a larger and more durable Retina display with thinner borders. It comes in two sizes: 41mm and 45mm.

  • Design:This Series has a design with softer, more rounded corners. It is water-resistant and has a durable aluminum case.

  • Health and Fitness:It includes various health and fitness features, such as heart rate monitoring, ECG app, blood oxygen measurement, sleep tracking, and a variety of workout tracking options.

  • Battery Life: The battery life can vary based on usage, but Apple claims all-day battery life.

  • Connectivity: It offers GPS and cellular connectivity options, allowing you to make calls and use data even when your Mobile-Phone is not nearby.

  • Compatibility:This Watch is designed to work seamlessly with Mobile-Phones.
